    </div>

    <div class="mt-4 space-y-4 text-muted leading-relaxed">

      <p>Trauma is not a sign of weakness. It is what happens when an experience is too much for the nervous system to process on its own.</p>
    </div>

      <p class="text-muted leading-relaxed">Healing doesn't mean forgetting. It means the memory loses its grip. You feel more present, more grounded, more like yourself. Less like you are always bracing for something.</p>

    </div>

    <div class="mt-4 space-y-4 text-muted leading-relaxed">

      <p>It might look like a loud inner critic, patterns you keep repeating, difficulty trusting people, or a body that holds tension you can't name. Many people don't connect their present struggles to their past. But the link is real, and it matters.</p>

      <p class="text-muted leading-relaxed">Understanding where your patterns come from changes your relationship to them. You start responding instead of reacting. The story of your past becomes something you carry differently, not something that carries you.</p>

    </div>

    <div class="mt-4 space-y-4 text-muted leading-relaxed">

      <p>Burnout is not laziness. It is what happens when you have given more than you had for too long, without enough to replenish it.</p>

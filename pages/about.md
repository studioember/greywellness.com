## Compassionate, evidence-based care for sleep, anxiety, and trauma.

**About Me**

I am a Latina immigrant, born in Bogotá and raised in Barrancabermeja, Colombia, and I built my life and career in the United States carrying everything that journey meant. That experience is not just a detail about me — it is at the heart of how I work. I understand what it means to straddle two worlds, to carry expectations that were never spoken out loud, and to spend years being strong before you ever learn it is okay not to be.

I became a psychotherapist because I believe that the things we carry from childhood, from our cultures, and from years of simply surviving deserve more than a coping strategy. They deserve real attention, real understanding, and real change.

**What I Specialize In**

Much of my work centers around sleep, trauma, and stress, not as separate boxes to check, but as deeply connected parts of how we function. Many of the people I work with are capable, high-achieving adults who look fine on the outside but are quietly exhausted. They have tried to push through. They are tired of just managing.

If you have spent years lying awake at night, replaying thoughts you cannot turn off, I work specifically with people struggling with insomnia, getting to the root of what is keeping you awake rather than just treating the symptom.

If you grew up in an environment that felt unpredictable, chaotic, or simply not safe enough, those early experiences have a way of showing up in adulthood in ways that can feel confusing. The anxiety, the perfectionism, the way you brace for things to go wrong — it all started somewhere. We work through it at a pace that feels manageable, with compassion and without judgment.

And if chronic stress or burnout has become your baseline, we work on practical, grounded ways to help you feel like yourself again, not a watered-down version of yourself just trying to get through the week.

**How I Work**

My foundation is Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, combined with mindfulness and a deep respect for who you are as a whole person. I am structured and goal-oriented, but therapy with me is never rigid or cold. I care about your progress and I also care about you. We will work hard together, and there will also be room for honesty, humor, and humanity.

I am direct. I value evidence. And I deeply respect the courage it takes to look at your own patterns and decide you want something different.

**Who I Work Best With**

I work with adults navigating unresolved childhood experiences, insomnia, chronic stress, and the particular weight that comes with immigration, cultural identity, and being first. I offer therapy in both English and Spanish, and for many of my Spanish-speaking clients, being able to do this work in their first language makes all the difference.

You do not have to keep functioning on fumes. You do not have to keep repeating the same patterns. And you do not have to do this alone. If you are ready to do something about it, I would be honored to work with you.

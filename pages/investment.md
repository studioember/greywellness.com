Investing in your mental health is one of the most meaningful decisions you can make, and cost should not have to be the thing that stands in the way. The good news is that many insurance plans include out-of-network benefits, which means a portion of your sessions may already be covered. Claiming those benefits is simpler than most people expect, and I am here to make it as easy as possible for you.

## Session Fees

I am an out-of-network provider, which means I do not bill insurance directly. Payment is due at the time of each session. A superbill is provided after each session, which you can submit directly to your insurance company for reimbursement.

## Using Your Insurance Benefits

To take the guesswork out of it, I partner with Mentaya to help you quickly verify your out-of-network coverage and navigate the reimbursement process. Use the tool below to check your benefits in just a few minutes.

## Sliding Scale

I believe financial barriers should not stand in the way of getting support. A limited number of sliding scale spots are available on a case-by-case basis. If cost is a concern, please don't hesitate to bring it up during our consultation and we can explore what works for you.

## Questions?

If you have any questions about finances, feel free to [reach out](/pages/contact). I am happy to talk it through before you commit to anything.

## Using Your Insurance

We are an out-of-network provider, which means we do not bill insurance directly. However, many insurance plans include out-of-network benefits that can cover a portion of your session cost. After each session, we provide a superbill you can submit to your insurance company for reimbursement.

## Sliding Scale

We believe cost should not be the reason someone doesn't get support. A limited number of reduced-fee spots are available on a case-by-case basis. If affordability is a concern, please bring it up during your free consultation and we will do our best to find something that works for you.

## Questions?

If you have any questions about fees or insurance, feel free to [reach out](/pages/contact). We are happy to talk it through before you commit to anything.
